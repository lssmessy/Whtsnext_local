Whtsnext is a social networking site which helps to connect the students across globe who had just finished their 10th,12th,graduate or post graduate degrees and in the confusion about what to do next? So, here the idea is simple: Sign up for your account for free!! Set the preferences (10th ,12th, Graduate or Post Graduate, Professional) And there you go!! You can also browse, search, and contact the person from whom you want have advice for your career! And by this way, we are trying to minimize the confusion of students who just passed out! Hope this initiative may help a little bit to the students!
